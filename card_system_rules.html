Persi - Card Battle Ruleset

Persi is the core card game played within the Crabs demo. The goal? Beat your opponent down to 0 health.

Basic Mechanics

  • Each card deals damage equal to its rank. For example, a 5 deals 5 damage. Max damage from a single card is 10.
  • Cards are placed on "dumps" (think stacks or zones). When a new card is placed, it's considered in an attacking state.
  • The opponent must respond by placing a matching card on top to block the attack:
    • Matching rank or suit is required to defend.
  • If the defending player can't respond, they take the full damage from the card.
  • After defending, the newly played card becomes the new attacker. It's an endless slap-fight until someone caves in.

Special Cards

Some cards come with spicy rules to mix up the game:

  • Ace (any suit): When attacking, can only be defended with another Ace.
  • Seven (any suit): Deals double damage when played. If stacked on top of another Seven, the damage just keeps getting better, because the multiplier keeps on rising.

That's the gist. It's a fast-paced, rule-light, damage-focused card game.
